About Us

Who Are We?

Chinaflower214 is the most professional local network florist in China. We provide the fastest floral delivery service, within 2 - 6 hours. We can design and create custom bouquets to fit your needs (bouquets for family, for friendship, for love, or birthday). We can also create a display of flowers for opening a commercial exhibition. In addition to providing fresh flowers, punctual delivery and satisfactory after-sales service, we will regularly launch the promotional and new products to fully satisfy the needs of customers in various occasions. With an efficient management team, high quality products, good service and honest business principles, Chinaflower214 has achieved a high level of repeat and referral business. In order to provide services to both domestic and overseas customers efficiently and promptly, we accept multiple methods of payment, such as international credit cards, online banking and other online payment methods.

6. We serve with love. In all transactions, your satisfaction is our top priority. Currently, we deliver only within mainland China.
