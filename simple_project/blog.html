Meaning Behind Colours of Roses

Red – Red is the color for romance. It stands for passion, love, desire, and commitment. Gift them to your romantic partner on Valentine’s Day or on a special occasion that you share in your relationship.

White – Youth and innocence is what the white roses represent. These roses are used as bridal flowers that signify youthfulness and young love. Loyalty is also what it stands for.

Lavender – This is a very enchanting color that is used to make a strong statement. People usually like to gift a bouquet of them on a theme party or for some unique event.

Most Popular Flowers

Roses-One of the most popular flowers in the world, a rose has thousands of varieties, colors, and hues.

Lilies-This tall beauty is also very admired for its looks. This showy flower is one of the most desired flowers in bouquets.

How to Arrange Bouquet Flowers

Take a pair of scissors and put criss-cross cuts on the stems of the flowers. This way, when you put them in water, you would get a much richer and the flowers would rise up more. Now, take a vase and fill it 3/4th with water. Cut the leaves off your flowers which you think may fall into the water.

Use your pair of scissors now to diagonally cut the stem at least by one inch. It is important to cut them diagonally. Now, put them in the vase that you filled with water. Put the flowers at an angle from each other so that they look carefully arranged and not like you just put them in water.
